This is Balu

Nestled in a lush valley, the village exudes a quiet charm that feels like a step back in time.
Cobblestone streets wind through clusters of quaint cottages, their thatched roofs dusted with a light mist that rises with the morning sun. Vibrant flowers bloom in every window box, their colors adding a splash of life against the rustic stone walls. The scent of fresh bread wafts from the bakery at the village square, where locals gather for morning gossip and to pick up their daily provisions.
